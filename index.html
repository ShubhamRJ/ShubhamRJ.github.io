A bit about me

Graduate student at Stony Brook University, studying Computer Science with a focus on distributed systems and language models.

I'm drawn to systems that need to work at scale — the kind where a single failure can cascade, where consensus is hard to achieve, and where the tradeoffs between consistency and availability are real and daily.

On the side, I've spent years working in frontend engineering, building code editors and interfaces that developers actually enjoy using. There's something deeply satisfying about crafting experiences that feel invisible — where the tool disappears and the thinking shines through.

I write publicly because it forces precision. A half-formed idea survives inside your head; it doesn't survive a blank page. When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm out with a camera — finding frames in everyday moments.
