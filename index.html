A bit about me

Software Engineer at Microsoft Azure building distributed systems for the planet-scale datacenter platform.

I'm drawn to systems that push the limits of scale--environments where "consensus" is a hard-won battle and the tug-of-war between consistency and availability is a daily reality. I don't just write code; I design systems built to survive the chaos of cascading failures.

When I step away from the terminal, I'm usually seeking perspective. Whether it's through the lens of a telescope, the pages of a philosophy text, or a camera frame on the streets of Seattle, I'm interested in the "big picture." To keep grounded, I run, hike, and hunt for the city's best espresso.

Up for a coffee chat? I'm always looking for new music and even better conversations. Let's connect.
