Peter Thiel has this famous question that he asks in interviews he takes - 
> <strong> What important truth do very few people agree with you on? </strong>
{: .prompt-tip }

My answers are obviously biased to my experiences so it may or may not be valid at the scale of world population. Nonetheless, here's an attempt to answer this question:

# Year 2025:
1. All philosophical questions have only 2 answers. Yes and No. Both are equally correct. Choosing 1 defines the life you want to lead.
2. Recency and observational bias play bigger role than we think in our general outlook toward life & future.
