# Personal Recommendations

My list of personal favourites of anything and everything at one place. These are highly curated channels and blogs which I have been following regularly for more than half a year. Also so that I can remove my infinite bookmarks 😛

Got a recommendation? Let me know!

---

## YouTube Channels

### Tech

- [Honeypot.io](https://www.youtube.com/@Honeypotio) — Their documentaries around tech's biggest projects are a very, very interesting watch.

### Science

- [Mark Rober](https://www.youtube.com/@MarkRober) — One of my favourite science creators. His videos are on another level!
- [VSauce](https://www.youtube.com/@Vsauce) — A creator I wish would be as active as before. He has answered some of the weirdest science questions. Watch his Mindfield series if you're interested in Philosophy and Psychology.
- [Kurzgesagt – In a Nutshell](https://www.youtube.com/@kurzgesagt) — Very cool illustrations about interesting questions in science.
- [Veritasium](https://www.youtube.com/@veritasium) — Needs no introduction.

### Other

- [RealLifeLore](https://www.youtube.com/@RealLifeLore) — One of the best channels to grow your geo-political and geo-economic knowledge. Highly recommended!
- [Improvement Pill](https://www.youtube.com/@ImprovementPill) — A well-subscribed channel for ideas on building your own personality.

## Blogs

### Tech Blogs

While the major top tech engineering blogs are always great reads — [Netflix](https://netflixtechblog.com/), [Uber](https://www.uber.com/blog/), [Google Research](https://ai.googleblog.com/) — these are the lesser-known ones I love:

- [Sumit's Diary](https://blog.reachsumit.com/) — Great research summaries and content on Recommendation Systems using LLMs.
- [Aman AI](https://aman.ai/recsys/papers/) — A great resource for research papers in Recommendation Systems and NLP.
- [web.dev](https://web.dev/) — My personal favourite frontend engineering resource for learning and reading use cases.

### Non-tech Blogs

- [Dan Koe](https://thedankoe.com/blog/) — Unique ideas about human potential, lifestyle design, and one-person businesses.
